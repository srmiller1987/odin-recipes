Bowl of Kibble

-------

Description

This is a very enticing bowl of dry, crunchy kibbles. No dog can resist its tantalizing crunch. They'll come running back for it time and time again!

Ingredients You'll Need:
  • One (1) bowl of appropriate size
  • About half a cup of kibbles
  • Optional but not really: One (1) handsome pill

Steps:
  1. Ask dog if he is hungry. (He always is, this is a stupid question.)
  2. Scoop half cup of kibbles with whatever implement you prefer.
  3. Give him a handsome pill.
  4. Place kibbles into the bowl and stand back as he crunches away.
