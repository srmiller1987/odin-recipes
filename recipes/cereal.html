Cereal in a Bowl

-------

Description

Who doesn't love a bowl of cereal? Morning, noon, or night, this one is a quick and easy one-bowl meal that anyone of any age can master in no time flat.

Ingredients

  • Bowl
  • Cereal of your choice
  • Milk (dairy or plant based, give it your own style!)
  • Spoon

Directions

  1. Assemble all ingredients and implements before starting.
  2. Pour desired amount of cereal into your bowl.
  3. Pour milk over the cereal. Stir to combine if desired.
  4. Consume and repeat until you either explode or run out of cereal.
