Eggs and Rice

-------

Description

A warm, comforting bowl of white rice and a runny-yolked egg on top is bound to make your day. This is a delicious meal for any time of day but it is best at breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Rice of your choosing
  • Number of eggs to fit your appetite
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: spicy chili crisp
  • Optional: soy sauce

Directions

  1. Cook your preferred rice and place into a bowl.
    • Optional if desired: Add spicy chili crisp and soy sauce and stir to combine
  2. Fry your eggs until the whites are just set.
  3. Place eggs on top of the rice and break the yolk into the rice.
  4. Eat for every single meal forever because it is so delicious.
